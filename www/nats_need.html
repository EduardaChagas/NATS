Data file

NATS accepts only CSV files that must present data related to a time series.

The data files can be loaded in the choose CSV files section and each line must represent the value of the series in a certain period of time to be evaluated.

Example of a time series data:

Daily temperature
   23
   20.5
   22
   30.5
   31
   27.5
   26.25
   26.5
   23
   23.5

Probability distribution

After uploading the data file, identify the probability distribution that should be used in the calculations of the descriptors.

Parameters of ordinal patterns

Choose the dimension and delay to be used in the formation of the ordinal patterns of the time series data. By default, NATS will preset dimension 3 and delay 1.
